# Community Response and Local Concerns

The incident sparked a strong emotional response across Edinburgh.

- **Safety concerns** — Many residents expressed anxiety about violent incidents in public spaces.
- **Frustration with rumours** — People criticised how quickly misinformation spread.
- **Support for emergency services** — Many praised the rapid police response.
- **Calls for clarity** — Residents asked for more transparent communication from authorities.

The event became a focal point for broader conversations about safety, mental health support, and public awareness.

# Timeline of the Edinburgh Knife Incident

## Early Reports
Initial posts on social media described a man armed with a knife in a public area. Information spread quickly, often with conflicting details.

## Emergency Response
Police and emergency services arrived shortly after the first calls. A cordon was established, and officers worked to secure the area.

## Public Updates
Authorities released brief statements confirming the incident and urging the public not to speculate. Despite this, online discussion intensified.

## Aftermath
As the situation came under control, official updates clarified the basic facts. Community concern remained high, and the incident continued to trend locally.

# How the Story Spread and Why It Became an “Obsession”

The incident became a major topic online for several reasons.

- **High visibility** — Incidents in central or busy areas spread quickly.
- **Uncertainty** — Lack of early official details created space for speculation.
- **Viral sharing** — Videos and posts circulated rapidly, often without context.
- **Emotional impact** — Fear-based stories tend to gain traction faster than routine news.

This combination created what many described as an “Edinburgh obsession,” with constant updates, theories, and commentary dominating local feeds.
